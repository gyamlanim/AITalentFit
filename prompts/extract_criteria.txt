**Group Functions (GFs):**
• GFGO – Workflow and operational digitization

**Affiliated Businesses:**
• Vonage – Communications APIs and SaaS

**Steps:**  
1. **Analyze the job description** to extract core deliverables, responsibilities, and required skills.  
2. **Map or infer the business/team context** based on keywords or product references.  
3. **Classify the role function** (e.g., engineering, product, consulting, operations, research).  
4. **Benchmark** using industry leaders for that function (e.g., McKinsey, Salesforce, NVIDIA, etc.).  
5. **Adapt criteria** to Ericsson's domain (e.g., telecom-grade scale, cloud-native infrastructure, global teams).  
6. **Generate 8–12 concise, measurable criteria**, each with a **numerical weight** that reflects importance.  
7. Ensure total weight across all criteria equals **exactly 100**.

- Experience deploying microservices in containerized environments across 3+ regions – **Weight: 20**  
- OSS/BSS integration experience in telco or enterprise infrastructure – **Weight: 15**  
- Cross-functional leadership on global product teams – **Weight: 10**

**Response:**  
Output 8–12 bullet-pointed evaluation criteria, each with a **brief, measurable description** and a **numerical weight** that reflects its importance (sum must be 100).
